MEET THE INSTRUCTOR

Ankur Warikoo

People often ask me - how do you get so much done in a day? You are an entrepreneur, an investor, a social media influencer, a fitness enthusiast, a public speaker, a voracious reader, a meditator, an aspiring singer. And I ask them back - what stops you from doing all of this? "Well, we don't have the time" is most often the response.

Guess what - that's a poor excuse. Because no one else has more time than you. Everyone has the SAME 24 HOURS!

Early on in life, I realized the importance of time. And how if I do not manage it, it will end up controlling me. Those active decisions have today become a habit and those habits have now become my life. It is these decisions, habits and techniques that I wish to share with all of you. To help you take control of your time as well.

Today, at 39, I have run 3 startups, raised $40Mn+, invested in 20+ startups, have a social media presence of 8.9Mn+ followers and am an active public speaker. FORTUNE India featured me in their 40 Under 40 list for India and am part of LinkedIn India's PowerProfiles list.

And all of these experiences are attributed to my relationship with time.
